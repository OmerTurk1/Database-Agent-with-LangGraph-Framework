## Problems

### Problem 1: Algebra
Solve for x:

1. 2x + 3 = 11
2. 5(x - 2) = 3x + 4

### Problem 2: Geometry
Calculate the area and perimeter of the following shapes:

1. Rectangle with length 5 cm and width 3 cm.
2. Circle with a radius of 4 cm.

### Problem 3: Trigonometry
If sin(θ) = 0.5, find the value of θ in degrees.

### Problem 4: Statistics
Given the following data set: 5, 7, 8, 9, 10, calculate the mean and median.
