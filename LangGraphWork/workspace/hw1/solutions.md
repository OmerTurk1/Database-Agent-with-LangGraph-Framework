# High School Mathematics Homework Solutions

## Problem 1: Algebra

### Solution 1:
Solve for x:

1. 2x + 3 = 11  
   2x = 11 - 3  
   2x = 8  
   x = 4

2. 5(x - 2) = 3x + 4  
   5x - 10 = 3x + 4  
   5x - 3x = 4 + 10  
   2x = 14  
   x = 7

## Problem 2: Geometry

### Solution:
1. Rectangle:  
   Area = length * width = 5 cm * 3 cm = 15 cm²  
   Perimeter = 2(length + width) = 2(5 cm + 3 cm) = 16 cm

2. Circle:  
   Area = π * radius² = π * (4 cm)² = 16π cm²  
   Perimeter (Circumference) = 2π * radius = 2π * 4 cm = 8π cm

## Problem 4: Statistics

### Solution:
Data set: 5, 7, 8, 9, 10  
Mean = (5 + 7 + 8 + 9 + 10) / 5 = 39 / 5 = 7.8  
Median = 8 (the middle value when arranged in order).
